# Nougat Research
This repository hosts code, documentation, and resources for Nougat Research and Nougat Media.

## What is Nougat Research?
Nougat Research is an independent research project dedicated to publishing work of public interest, freely available to everyone. Its focus areas include technology, media, and public affairs.
All research published under Nougat Research is free to read and use. Any code is open source.

## What is Nougat Media?
Nougat Media is a separate but related project that produces editorial content; primarily articles and visual explainers, based on research developed by Nougat Research.

## Contributing
Nougat Research is currently a one-person project. Contributions, corrections, and feedback are welcome. If you spot something worth investigating or an error worth fixing, feel free to open an issue or a pull request.
